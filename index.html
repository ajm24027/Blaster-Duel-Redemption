How To Play

At the beginning of each duel, a pattern appears in the duel area and disappears after a certain time. The player has a time limit to input the correct pattern into their blaster.

If they do, the player successfully loads their blaster with the correct pattern and hits their mark, winning the duel. Players use console controls, consisting of two large buttons (orange and blue), to input the pattern into their blaster by clicking the corresponding circle to fill the next available slot. They continue clicking until the pattern is complete or the time is up.

A retro style, pattern based, first person Shooter set in a galaxy far away.
