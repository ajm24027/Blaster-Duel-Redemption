How To Play

  • Become the fastest gun in Wild Space by dueling evil Space Barons who have faster and faster draws.
  • Study the pattern that appears at the top of the duel area and use the console controls (orange and blue buttons) to input the correct pattern into your blaster before the time runs out.
  • Inputting the correct pattern lets you hit your mark to win the duel and move on to the next round with a new, more difficult pattern.
  • Defeat every Baron who challenges you to keep your family's land
A retro style, pattern based, first person Shooter set in a galaxy far away.
